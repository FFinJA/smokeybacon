About Us

Our Story

Our journey began with our specialty—Smokey Bacon Jam—which our founder made and sold from her home kitchen. As the small operation grew into a successful business, the need to expand our horizons became apparent.

In 2023, Smokey Bacon opened in Downtown Montreal to bring you our famous bacon jam, as well as an expanded menu full of mouth-watering bacon dishes. From appetizers to desserts, you will find a nice variety to satisfy your bacon cravings.

Every meal comes with a sample of our signature Smokey Bacon Jam. If you love it as much as we do, we sell plenty of different formats if you wish to bring some home.
